The UX Designer

Name: Sarah Lusher
Age: 24
Bio: As a UX designer, I continually practice putting myself in other people's shoes, as I believe that is the best way to getting to know your user's and client's need's.
I like to explore all possibilities and not just settle on the first option, therefore continue to ask myself, "Is this the best I can do?" until I have created something I truly believe will benefit others.

History

Frontløberne

Who?: Frontløberne are an open project platform for the young community between 19-35 years of age. It's a very creative and friendly place with a strong sense of community.
‍
The goal: Re-create their webpage so it gave a better reflection of the organisation and community and was more user friendly. Also add content that is more necessary for the user and therefore attract them to visit the website more often.

Create social media content that would encourage user generated content and increase the interest and awareness of Frontløberne to their users.
